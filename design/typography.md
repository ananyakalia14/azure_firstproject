# Typography

## Recommended Fonts
- Headings: Inter or Segoe UI
- Body: Inter or Open Sans
- Monospace: Consolas or JetBrains Mono

## Scale
- H1: 32px
- H2: 24px
- H3: 20px
- Body: 16px
- Caption: 12px

## Guidelines
Use a consistent hierarchy to improve readability and maintain a polished product experience.
